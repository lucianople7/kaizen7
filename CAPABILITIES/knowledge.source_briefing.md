# knowledge.source_briefing

## Purpose

Convert source packs into structured briefs, claims, citations and content angles for dossiers, scripts and Flowmatik videos.

## Use When

- A mission includes long sources, pasted research, papers, competitors, suppliers or product information.
- THE FOCUX needs a dossier or evidence-led content.
- Mr. Kaizen needs a script grounded in sources.

## Inputs

- `source_pack`
- `objective`
- `audience`
- `claims_policy`

## Outputs

- `briefing`
- `claims`
- `citations`
- `content_angles`
- `risks`
- `next_action`

## Providers

- Manual Obsidian review.
- Future Open Notebook / NotebookLM-like provider.
- Future local RAG provider.

## Verification

- Sources are present.
- Claims are extracted.
- Risks are reported.
- Citations are visible.
