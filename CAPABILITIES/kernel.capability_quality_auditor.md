# kernel.capability_quality_auditor

## Purpose

Score KAIZEN7 capabilities against quality gates so the registry improves by evidence instead of volume.

## Use When

- The registry grows.
- A capability feels vague.
- KAIZEN7 needs to decide what to improve next.
- A new capability is proposed and needs review.

## Do Not Use When

- The task needs execution.
- The mission needs product, content or code work directly.
- Human approval is required before judging quality.

## Outputs

- quality report
- scores
- improvements
- next action

## Verification

- Quality report is present.
- Scores are present.
- Improvements are present.
- Lowest scoring capabilities are visible.
