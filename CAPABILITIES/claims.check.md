# claims.check

## Purpose

Check product, supplement and wellness claims before content or commerce use.

## Use When

- A mission mentions nootropics, supplements, ingredients, health, performance, sleep, stress, cognition or ecommerce claims.

## Outputs

- allowed claims
- blocked claims
- risk notes
- source requirements

## Hard Blocks

- Medical treatment claims.
- Guaranteed results.
- Disease prevention/cure language.
- Unsupported supplement performance claims.
- NEUROCITY metaphor presented as biology.
