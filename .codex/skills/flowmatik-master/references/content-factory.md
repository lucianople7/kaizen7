# Content Factory

## Purpose

Create high-leverage content systems for Flowmatik, THE FOCUX and other ventures. Favor repeatable campaign engines over isolated posts.

## Core Content Pack

For each campaign, produce:

- 10 hooks
- 3 short-form video scripts
- 1 carousel outline
- 5 story prompts
- 3 ad angles
- 1 landing/ecommerce CTA
- 1 email or DM sequence

## Short-Form Video Structure

Use this default structure:

1. Hook in 0-3 seconds.
2. Pain or status signal.
3. Mechanism or insight.
4. Proof, demonstration or contrast.
5. CTA that matches funnel stage.

For THE FOCUX, avoid hype and gym energy. Use precision, control, long hours, responsibility, real doses and premium transparency.

## Flowmatik Style

- Direct
- Operational
- Built for repeated output
- Converts ideas into scripts, edits, captions and campaigns
- Prefer practical language over motivational filler

## Output Rules

- Always name the channel: TikTok, Reels, Shorts, LinkedIn, email or ecommerce.
- Include CTA.
- Include production notes when video is involved.
- Add variants for premium, educational and direct tones when useful.
