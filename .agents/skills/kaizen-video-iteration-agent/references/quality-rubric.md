# Kaizen Video Quality Rubric

Score each category from 1 to 5.

## Categories

- **Hook**: the first 3 seconds create curiosity without needing context.
- **Rhythm**: cuts, scene duration, motion, and music direction feel intentional.
- **Visual Identity**: the piece is recognizably Kaizen/Flowmatik without needing a logo.
- **Clarity**: the viewer understands the idea, emotional promise, and next beat.
- **Emotional Pull**: it makes the world feel worth entering.
- **Technical Finish**: aspect ratio, text placement, resolution, audio, and transitions are clean.
- **Reusability**: assets and decisions can become a repeatable format.

## Score Meaning

- **1**: broken or unclear
- **2**: usable only as rough internal material
- **3**: solid concept test
- **4**: strong demo candidate
- **5**: publish/client-ready

## Improvement Priority

Choose the lowest-scoring category that also has high leverage. For early drafts, prioritize:

1. Hook
2. Rhythm
3. Clarity
4. Visual identity
5. Technical finish

Do not chase polish before the concept is watchable.
