# Research Intelligence

## When To Research

Use live research when the answer depends on current facts:

- Competitors
- Prices
- Manufacturers
- Regulations
- Ingredients and claims
- Ecommerce trends
- Tool availability
- Market size
- News or recent product launches

## Research Workflow

1. Define the business question.
2. Identify 3-5 primary source types: official docs, manufacturer pages, retailer listings, regulatory pages, scientific papers, app/tool docs.
3. Gather current facts and links.
4. Separate facts from inference.
5. Produce recommendation and uncertainty.

## Market Report Format

- Question
- Short answer
- Evidence table
- Competitor patterns
- Opportunity
- Risks
- Next action

## Supplier/Manufacturer Search

Capture:

- Location
- Certifications
- MOQ
- Formats supported
- Ingredient sourcing
- Lead times
- Contact route
- Red flags

Do not present a manufacturer as vetted unless actual due diligence has been done.
