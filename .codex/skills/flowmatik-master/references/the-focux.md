# THE FOCUX

## Positioning

THE FOCUX is a premium cognitive and physical performance brand for people with real responsibility. It is not a generic supplement brand, cheap energy product, gym supplement, or inflated multivitamin.

Core thesis: fewer formulas, stronger execution. Prefer 1-3 potent products over a large mediocre catalog.

## Audience

- Programmers
- Entrepreneurs
- Executives
- Professional drivers
- Traders
- People aged 35-60 who need sustained clarity
- Customers with money, responsibility and long working hours

Avoid targeting students looking for cheap stimulation.

## Brand Rules

- Matte black and metallic gold visual system.
- Minimal, elegant, exclusive.
- Feel closer to Swiss watch, luxury car and serious biohacking than gym supplement.
- Message: real doses, premium raw materials, total transparency.
- Avoid "placebo marketing", proprietary blends and 20 underdosed ingredients.

## Claims Discipline

Do not write disease claims or guaranteed medical outcomes. Use careful language: "designed for", "supports", "positioned for", "candidate formula", "to validate with manufacturer/regulatory expert".

For exact doses, manufacturing cost, legality, EFSA/European claims or safety: research current regulations and validate with qualified specialists.
